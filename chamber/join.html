Thinking of Joining the Ogden Chamber of Commerce?

The Chamber of Commerce in general was founded to help businesses connect.

There are 4 levels of membership.

  • Bronze: $5/mo.
    • Gives you full access to our chamber of commerce web page, allowing you to see events and announcements about upcoming seminars, business meet-ups, and opportunities to connect within the community.
    • A page all to yourself where your business information can be shown for others to learn more about you.
  • Silver: $100/mo.
    • Includes all of Bronze benefits.
    • Opens your business up to discounts at the seminars (easily over $150 in savings if utilized).
    • Gives the option to allow inter city connecting, allowing you to use our tools to connect with other relevant business to get the best prices on product, shipping, and services as needed.
  • Gold: $200/mo.
    • Includes all of Silver benefits.
    • We will list your company on the main website and put you at the top of our lists when other businesses are looking for who to connect with.
    • You will be admitted into our meetings to talk about the future of businesses in Ogden
  • Non-Profit: Free*
    • Allows special access to our Non-Profit conections page.
    • Non-profit calendar available as well as the option to add your own events.
    • A page all to yourself explaining your Non-profit's cause and contact information, or whatever else you may like. Contact Us if you have any questions about how to set this up.

*requires proof of non-profit status
