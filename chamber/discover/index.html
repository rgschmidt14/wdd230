A little about what happens in Ogden

Christmas Village

Every year the day after Thanksgiving the downtown in Ogden is filled with light and a beautiful Christmas village, perfect to take a date or explore with your family. Dress Warm!

Hiking in Ogden

So many trails, ask anyone their favorite. More than likely they will have a few!

Ogden Historical Arch Sign

This iconic sign is over Washington Blvd, the central street in Ogden. Look for it as you are driving around. When you've seen it, you know you're at the heart of Ogden.

Weber State University Softball and The Dee Events Center

Weber State shares several of their facilities with the neighborhood, and none is more visited than the Dee Events Center! Be it for a basketball game, devotional, derby, or even a high school graduation. So many people have been inside of this building.

Pioneer Days Rodeo

The rodeo is in tandem with the Pioneer days celebration every 24th of July. Sign your kids up for mutton bustin'! Heck, sign yourself up for something! People come from near and far to set records and compete at this entertaining, edge-of-your-seat event!
